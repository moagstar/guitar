NOTES

This is my basic interpretation in tab form of the various phrases
of the song, I don't think it's 100% accurate but is enough to get started -
if you listen to the recording you can hear Nic adds lots of variation to
these basic phrases which gives the song such wonderful depth. He mixes up
it up a lot, sometimes plucking, sometimes hammering/pulling, or bending
for the same few notes in a phrase as it is repeated in the song. This
variation exists in the lyrics too "into a rage" / "too in a rage".

"x" denotes the rythmic flicking - this will take some practise, but feels
quite natural after you build up the muscle memory. Use your ring finger
to flick the string close to the bridge. Again there is variation here,
sometimes Nic lets the flick sound a muted note (don't let the flicking finger
rest and mute the string) but mostly it's a kind of muted percussive sound you
are after (flick and hold against the string to stop it from ringing, there is
a sweet spot close to the bridge where you find the "click" sound you are
looking for). Most often you will be flicking the 3rd string (F), you can see
in the tab where I think a different string is flicked. The left hand mostly
stays wherever it happens to be at the time during the flick.
